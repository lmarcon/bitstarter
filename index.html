/ˈkwaɪəʳ/ will change the way you learn your music sheets. The multi track studio helps directors to deliver single lines parts, letting the singer listen them separate or all together.

Often choir directors provide to choir members recorded tracks of themselves playing the vocal line with piano, or even singing the single parts. This is useful and effective, but limited, and can be improved with today technology.

For directors: imagine that with just a clic you can deliver entire portions of music sheets to all members of your choir.

For choir members: easily learn music by listening at your single vocal line only, or all lines together

FAQ

What does /ˈkwaɪəʳ/ stand for?
/ˈkwaɪəʳ/ is the pronunciation of 'Choir', written in phonetic language. Hence the name 'Kwaier', which almost have the same pronunciation of 'Choir'.
Which platforms are supported?
Initially Kwaier will be available online. Every updated browser will be able to use the software. After this first version, we're thinking about a mobile application.
